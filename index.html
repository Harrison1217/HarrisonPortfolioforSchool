Time in Service

As an Operation Specliest 3rd class in the Navy, my main responsabilitys were to track and report ships and vessels around our ship while we were underway(Out to sea). As a 3rd class I was in chrage of running the watch team. The team consisted of a Tracker, Chart Plotter, and Bridge Tracker. The Tracker was inside of the Combat Information center. They were responisble for tracking ships that were around our ship while we where in transit. The Chart plotter would take fixes on a chart and mke sure that we followed the course layed out by the Navagation Officer and the Quarter Masters. The Bridge tracker was responsible for passing course and speed changes to the tracker in combat where he would record the changes in a log. As the surface warfare supervisor I was incharge of making sure they were preforming their dutys. I was also responisble for getting reports ready for officers showing what has occured during our watch.
